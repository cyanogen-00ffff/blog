title: Restarting in Anime
date: 2026-07-06
---
I like anime. The problem is there are so many ideas and idioms surrounding it that has polluted my idea of the artform. That's why sometime last week, I decided to 'reset' my taste in anime, and rediscover it. To be honest, what I think I'm about to do is just rewatch some of my favorite shows and see if I still love them. If I do, then I'll branch out from there. Two of these are Yuru Yuri and Tantei Opera Milky Holmes. It seems they're the kind that's popular in Japan, and only there. It's not widely discussed overseas.

I feel my taste is adjacent to Japanese people in that they're more attuned to moe. I guess that's why all this time, when someone talks about anime, my mental image of it was always so different from how most people I talk to perceive it.
